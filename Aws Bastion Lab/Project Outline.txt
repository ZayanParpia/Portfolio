AWS-Native Virtual Home Cybersecurity Lab

Project Description (First-Person)

For this project, I will build a fully AWS-native, cloud-hosted virtual home lab to simulate real-world cybersecurity scenarios. I will design a VPC with public and private subnets, deploy EC2 instances as attacker and target machines, and configure Security Groups and NACLs to control traffic. I will enable VPC Flow Logs, CloudTrail, and CloudWatch to monitor activity, and use GuardDuty and Inspector to detect suspicious behavior and vulnerabilities.

This lab will allow me to safely practice attacks on vulnerable targets, test firewall rules and routing, and respond to simulated incidents. By the end, I will have a hands-on understanding of cloud network security, monitoring, and incident response—all using AWS-native tools.
